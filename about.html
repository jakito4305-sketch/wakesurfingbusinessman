About Me

Faith, work ethic, leadership, and steady growth

I am a student at Brigham Young University currently studying pre-business. I value discipline, meaningful relationships, and consistent improvement in both school and life.

My background has helped me grow in communication, responsibility, and leadership. Through work, service, and school, I have learned how important it is to be dependable, coachable, and willing to serve other people.

One of the most meaningful experiences in my life was serving as a volunteer representative in Brazil. That experience strengthened my confidence, helped me become fluent in Portuguese, and taught me how to connect with people from different backgrounds.

Personal Interests

Outside of school, I enjoy football, guitar, weightlifting, snowboarding, wakesurfing, and traveling. I also enjoy learning through new experiences and pushing myself in ways that build character.
